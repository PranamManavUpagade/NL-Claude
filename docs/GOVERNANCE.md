# Governance & Ethics — Calibrate

Purpose: operationalize ethics, retention, and audits.

Policies
- Data retention: default 30 days for AI outputs and traces (configurable).
- Consent: explicit consent required for data used in research or coach training.
- Anonymization: user identifiers removed for reports.

Operational rhythm
- Daily: guardrail health checks
- Weekly: CAR, completion, triage
- Monthly: opacity & control audits
- Quarterly: ethics review and retention policy review

Audit artifacts
- Opacity audit checklist
- Calibration drift metrics
- Dataset of flagged false positives/negatives

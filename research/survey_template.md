# Pilot / Research Survey — Calibrate

Purpose: collect quantitative signals about evaluation behavior and trust.

Sections:
1. Demographics (role, frequency of AI use)
2. Typical use cases (research, writing, career, other)
3. Scales (Likert 1–5)
   - How confident are you in AI outputs before checking? (1–5)
   - How often do you verify claims from AI outputs? (1–5)
   - How likely are you to act on an AI output without verification? (1–5)
4. Short scenarios: present a short AI snippet and ask whether they'd use it, what they'd check, and stakes.
5. Optional: contact for follow-up interview

Use Google Forms / Typeform. Aim for n≥30 for Phase 1.
